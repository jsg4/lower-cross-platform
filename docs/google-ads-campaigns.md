## Landing Page Requirements

Every ad should drive to a dedicated landing page, not the homepage. Key elements:

**Above the fold:**
- Headline matching the ad's promise (not generic)
- One-line sub: "We install growth infrastructure into $10M–$100M brands"
- CTA: "Get Your Free Profit Leak Report"
- Trust bar: Meta Partner | Google Partner | Forbes | $2B Revenue Generated

**Body:**
- The problem (3 bullets on why platform metrics lie)
- Your system (OS screenshot/mockup, 3 pillars: Technology + Operators + Accountability)
- Proof (2-3 anonymized results with real numbers)
- What the Profit Leak Report includes (3-4 bullet points)
- Form: Name, email, website, monthly ad spend (qualifier), biggest challenge (dropdown)

**Spend qualifier on the form filters out unqualified leads.** Dropdown options for monthly spend:
- Under $25K/mo (auto-disqualify or nurture)
- $25K–$100K/mo
- $100K–$500K/mo
- $500K+/mo

## Tracking Setup

- Google Ads conversion tracking on form submission
- Enhanced conversions enabled
- GA4 linked with Google Ads
- Offline conversion import for closed deals (feed back revenue data to optimize bidding)
- Call tracking if you add a phone number

## Week 1 Launch Checklist

1. Set up conversion tracking before launching any ads
2. Launch Campaigns 1 + 2 first (highest intent)
3. Campaign 3 (competitor) add in week 2 after baseline data
4. Campaign 4 (brand) launch immediately — cheap protection
5. Campaign 5 (retargeting) launch once you have 500+ site visitors
6. Review search terms report daily for first 2 weeks — add negatives aggressively
7. Pause any keyword with 100+ impressions and 0 clicks after 2 weeks
8. Target: 5-15 qualified leads/month at $150-300 CPL
